Our Restaurant

Cowbell Kitchen is about fresh, local, and fun. The Cowbell team works with like-minded local farmers, producers, and suppliers to create fresh, value-driven and fun appetizers, shared plates, lunch and dinner menus, and remarkable wood-fired pizza. Eat, drink and enjoy in Cowbell’s beautiful indoor and outdoor venues, subject to availability. Menu changes seasonally.

COME SEE US IN BLYTH!

Cowbell is a fully accessible, family-friendly destination brewery in Blyth at the corner of Highways 4 and 25. We are honoured to be the world's first closed-loop brewery, and the first carbon neutral brewery in North America.
